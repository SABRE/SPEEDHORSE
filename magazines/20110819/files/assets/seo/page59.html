By Diane M. Ciarloni

It’s nearly impossible to be anything but excited when a leading jockey such as G.R. Carter takes the time to sing the praises of your horse.

Carter rode Feature Mr Bojangles in his second career out on June 19, winning the 350-yard Four Corners Futurity at SunRay Park. Afterwards, Carter asked trainer/co-owner Roberto Sanchez (SM Cattle Co.) about the future plans he and Jaime Dominguez, Sanchez’s partner, had for the colt. They told him Feature Mr Bojangles, bred by Joyce Erickson in Texas, would ship to Arapahoe Park.

“I drove G.R. to the airport,” recalled Dominguez. “He few in on someone’s private plane and, just before he boarded, he told me the colt should go to the Rainbow. He said he was plenty good enough to make it. He also told me he had four horses to ride in the trials and Feature Mr Bojangles would be the ffth. That was all we needed to hear.

“We felt confdent we could go to Arapahoe and win some money with him but, after listening to G.R., we decided we needed to take the risk in the Rainbow.”
