By Julie Weathers, Speedhorse Contributor

When you’re 106 years old, victories are even sweeter, friendships are more precious and good horses are more appreciated. “I can truthfully say we owe a lot of our success to our trainer, Monty Arossa,” said Denny Weigt, who is half of the Weigt racing team in Jerome, Idaho. The other half is his partner and wife, Sandy. “Monty has not only been a great trainer, but he’s a good friend. Whenever we go to the track and there are some kids around he asks them, ‘Have you ever seen someone 106 years old?’ They always shake their heads and then he points at me. ‘That old man over there is.’ I’m not quite that old, I’m just 78, but it makes me laugh. “We work as a team from start to fnish.
